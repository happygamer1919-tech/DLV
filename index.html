Services

From standard truckload to engineered heavy haul, DLV builds the move around the cargo and the risk, not the other way around.

Truckload and LTL

Dry van, reefer, deck, and more across Canada and the United States. Regular lanes or last minute solves, covered with live updates and no guesswork.

Heavy haul and projects

Oversize and overweight freight with permits, escorts, and route checks built in. We coordinate with plants, yards, and job sites so everyone is ready when the unit arrives.

High value and regulated

Sensitive cargo that needs the right carriers and tight process. Temperature control, secure freight, and regulated commodities handled with documented steps and clear communication.

Cross border Canada and the US
